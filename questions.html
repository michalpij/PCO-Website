Frequently asked questions

Are you keen to find out how to book a project space at the HZ University? Maybe you're wondering how to access the web shop? Below you will find answers to these and other questions.

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First you need to connect with the printer. This used to be quite easy, however now you will probably need a new driver on your device. After downloading the driver, simply select the desired pages and send them to the printer. Then you will have to go to the printer, scan your HZ pass and print out your documents. The printing isn't free so remember to put money on your card. This can be done at the dedicated points at HZ, one of them is located at the ground level, across from the room with the coffee machine.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

To scan a document you will need your desired document and an hz pass. Remember to put money on the pass, since scanning, just like printing, isn't free.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

First go to the hz webpage. There, using your hz account, you can log in to your myhz profile. Next click on the webshop button in your profile and choose your purchase.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick?

The latest information regarding covid-19 can be fount on the hz webpage.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

Project spaces can be booked by using the SelfService Portal. Simply log in with your HZ account and choose the room that you want!

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

The parking spaces are located very close to the school. After passing the barriers next to the school, follow the signs to the dedicated HZ parking spaces. These spaces are marked with a white sign with the HZ logo. It is also possible to charge a couple of electric cars there. From there you only have to walk about 300 meters to get to the school. REMEMBER! You need a HZ pass to enter the parking lot.
