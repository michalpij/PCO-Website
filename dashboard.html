The NBSA, also known as the Negative Binding Study Advice, means that you won't be permitted to re-register for this study in the following years. In other words, if you dont acquire enough EC's, then you will fail the year wthout the possibility to re-enroll. The NBSA for the HBO-ICT study programme has been set to 45 EC's, meaning that you have to earn atleast 45 EC's to pass the year. Below you will find a table with all the necesseray information.
